Many people focus on what they don’t have. To them, success, happiness and self worth are based on external factors.

They would succeed, they would be happy, they would be worthy… If only they could get this next thing, if only they could get lucky just this one time.

You can wait for the stars to align. Or you can take responsibility. It’s up to you.
