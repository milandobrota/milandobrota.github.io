People don’t care about features. They don’t care about what you did. What they want to know instead is how the thing you built can help them.

They care about benefits, the meaning, the why. What is the purpose, the problem you’re trying to solve? Talking about the solution without mentioning the problem is confusing, dry and boring.

It’s not about you. It’s about the people you serve.
