A tool is useless and sometimes even dangerous if you don’t know how to use it properly. The what without the how is superficial, static and boring.

But the how alone is not enough.

The why separates mechanic from inspiring. The why gives us perspective, hope, vision, meaning, motivation, and purpose. The why crosses the line between reality and our dreams, it reveals a part of our personality, our goals and desires.

At times our actions can be difficult to understand. Don’t let that scare you. Finding out the reason behind your actions will tell you who you really are and what you stand for.
