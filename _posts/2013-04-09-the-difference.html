Fast is good.
Fast means bad quality.

Stable is good.
Stable is boring.

Fun is good.
Fun is time wasting.

Slow is safe.
Slow means missing out.

Adventure is exciting.
Adventure is dangerous.

A lot of things in life have a good and a bad side. It’s what you focus on that makes all the difference.
