Drive is your ultimate resource. You might not be competent, but if you want it bad enough you’ll get where you want to be. The current situation might be less than ideal, but hey, that’s the world we live in. The good news is that if you try hard enough, you will find a way to make it work. You don’t need to be the best or have the best, just make the best of what you have. And lastly.. the only person you need to compete with is yourself. Don’t obsess about what others do or have. Some people are more likely to make a decision if they see a lot of other people doing the same. Don’t worry about that. Think for yourself. It’s your life. What you make of it is up to you and only you.
